<p data-start="815" data-end="1590"><strong data-start="815" data-end="837">Market Definition:</strong><strong data-start="838" data-end="887">Electronic Clinical Outcome Assessment (eCOA)</strong> solutions refer to digital tools and software systems designed to collect, manage, and analyze clinical data during clinical trials or patient care processes. eCOA platforms enable the real-time capture of patient-reported outcomes (PROs), clinician-reported outcomes (ClinROs), and observer-reported outcomes (ObsROs) electronically. These platforms provide accurate, consistent, and reliable data, reducing the risk of human error, enhancing data integrity, and improving patient engagement. The shift from paper-based assessments to electronic systems has become increasingly important to streamline the clinical trial process, ensuring better data accuracy and compliance with regulatory standards.</p>
