<p data-start="736" data-end="1395"><strong data-start="736" data-end="758">Market Definition:</strong><strong data-start="759" data-end="777">Contrast media</strong>, also known as contrast agents, are substances used in medical imaging procedures to enhance the visibility of specific areas within the body, such as blood vessels, organs, and tissues. They are commonly used in procedures like CT scans, MRIs, X-rays, and ultrasound to improve the clarity and diagnostic capabilities of the imaging. Contrast media can be either iodine-based, gadolinium-based, or other types, depending on the imaging technology used. These agents help to provide more detailed, clearer images, aiding in the detection of abnormalities such as tumors, cardiovascular conditions, and other diseases.</p>
