<p data-start="812" data-end="834"><strong data-start="812" data-end="834">Market Definition:</strong></p>
<p data-start="836" data-end="1330">Narcolepsy is a neurological disorder that affects the brain's ability to regulate the sleep-wake cycle. Individuals with narcolepsy experience sudden, uncontrollable episodes of sleep during the day, along with symptoms such as cataplexy, sleep paralysis, and hallucinations. The <strong data-start="1117" data-end="1151">narcolepsy therapeutics market</strong> focuses on the development and commercialization of drugs and therapies to alleviate the symptoms and manage the disorder, improving the quality of life for affected individuals.</p>
