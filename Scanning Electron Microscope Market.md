<p data-start="677" data-end="699"><strong data-start="677" data-end="699">Market Definition:</strong></p>
<p data-start="701" data-end="1142">A <strong data-start="703" data-end="741">scanning electron microscope (SEM)</strong> is an advanced microscope that uses electrons instead of light to generate high-resolution images of the surface of a sample. SEMs provide highly detailed, magnified images, which can be used to analyze the surface morphology, texture, and composition of various materials. They are widely used in fields such as material science, nanotechnology, semiconductor manufacturing, and biological research.</p>
