<p data-start="689" data-end="1251"><strong data-start="689" data-end="711">Market Definition:</strong> <strong data-start="712" data-end="740">Hip replacement implants</strong> refer to the medical devices used to replace a damaged or worn-out hip joint. Hip replacement surgeries are typically performed to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore the function of the hip in patients suffering from severe arthritis or hip fractures. The implants consist of a prosthetic ball and socket joint, and they are usually made from materials like metal, plastic, or ceramic. These implants are crucial in restoring a patient's quality of life by providing better movement and reducing pain.</p>
