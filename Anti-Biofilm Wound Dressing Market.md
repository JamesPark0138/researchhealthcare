<p data-start="733" data-end="1350"><strong data-start="733" data-end="755">Market Definition:</strong><strong data-start="756" data-end="788">Anti-biofilm wound dressings</strong> are advanced dressings designed to prevent or eliminate biofilms in chronic wounds. Biofilms are clusters of bacteria embedded in a protective layer of extracellular polymeric substances, making the bacteria more resistant to traditional treatments like antibiotics. Chronic wounds, such as diabetic foot ulcers and surgical wounds, are highly susceptible to biofilm formation, which complicates the healing process. Anti-biofilm wound dressings are specifically formulated to disrupt the biofilm structure, promoting better healing and reducing infection risk.</p>
