<p data-start="692" data-end="1368"><strong data-start="692" data-end="714">Market Definition:</strong><strong data-start="715" data-end="734">Pressure ulcers</strong>, also known as bedsores or decubitus ulcers, are injuries to the skin and underlying tissues caused by prolonged pressure on the skin. These ulcers commonly occur in individuals who are bedridden or immobilized for long periods, such as elderly patients or those with chronic illnesses. Pressure ulcer treatment includes various methods aimed at relieving pressure, promoting healing, preventing infection, and managing the discomfort and pain associated with these wounds. The treatment options include wound dressings, therapeutic mattresses, surgical interventions, and advanced biologic therapies, which are continually evolving.</p>
