<p data-start="692" data-end="1379">Carbon dioxide incubators are specialized equipment used to grow cell cultures in a controlled environment with precise levels of temperature, humidity, and carbon dioxide (CO2) concentrations. These incubators are widely used in various applications, including research in molecular biology, microbiology, cell culture, and the production of therapeutic products. By regulating CO2 concentrations and maintaining a stable environment, CO2 incubators create optimal conditions for cell growth and division. The growing demand for advanced life sciences research and biopharmaceutical manufacturing is expected to boost the market for carbon dioxide incubators during the forecast period.</p>
