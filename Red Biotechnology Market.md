<p data-start="607" data-end="1252">Red biotechnology refers to the application of biotechnological processes to medical and health-related fields. Red biotechnology is instrumental in the production of vaccines, therapeutic proteins, gene therapies, cell therapies, and advanced biologics for the treatment of chronic diseases, genetic disorders, and infectious diseases. As demand for personalized medicine and advanced therapeutic solutions continues to rise, the red biotechnology market is expected to grow substantially.</p>
